SECTOR LANGUAGE ADVANTAGE
// REAL OBJECTIONS. REAL ANSWERS.
"We can build our own extraction in-house."
What's your current estimated cost to build to 99.5% accuracy across 11,500+ financial sources? And what does your maintenance roadmap look like as source formats change? I can help you model the build vs. buy comparison — most companies find the 18-month cost of building exceeds Dext's API cost by 3–5× before they're at parity on accuracy.
"We already have an OCR or extraction vendor."
What's your current accuracy rate on unstructured documents — handwritten receipts, non-standard invoice formats, non-English source documents? Dext processes 700K+ businesses worth of data. The accuracy difference often shows up in edge cases. I'd like to run a comparison on a sample of your actual documents.
"This isn't a priority right now."
Understood. Can I ask — what percentage of your current document processing errors are costing you in manual review time or customer escalations? I ask because most companies I speak to discover the extraction problem is costing more than it looks. I'm not looking to rush a decision — I'd rather show you the data and let you decide when it becomes urgent.
"We don't have budget right now."
Budget or priority? Those are different conversations. If it's priority, I'd rather understand what's ahead of this internally so I can help you make the case when the window opens. If it's genuinely budget, let me show you how this typically gets funded — usually under infrastructure or data quality, not a new line item.
